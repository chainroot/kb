# Distribution Module in Cosmos Network

The Distribution module is a vital component of the Cosmos network, managing the distribution of staking rewards and transaction fees. This document explores the Distribution module's functionalities and its importance in incentivizing network participation.

## Overview of the Distribution Module
- **Purpose**: Manages the distribution of rewards and fees to validators and delegators in the Cosmos network.
- **Functionality**: Ensures fair and efficient distribution of network incentives.

## Key Functionalities
- **Reward Distribution**: Automates the process of distributing staking rewards to validators and delegators.
- **Fee Allocation**: Manages the distribution of transaction fees within the network.

## Usage and Examples

- **Fee Distribution Mechanics**: Explanation of how transaction fees are distributed among participants.

## Best Practices
